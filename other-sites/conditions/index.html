Privacy & Conditions

Local Storage

This web application uses the browser's localStorage to keep your name and chat history. This means your data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly share it.

Third Party APIs

Akira AI uses the Google Gemini API to generate responses. When you chat, your messages are sent to Google's servers for processing according to their privacy policies.

No Accounts

We do not use an account system. If you clear your browser's site data or cache, your history and name will be lost.
